About Us

Queen Capital International Limited “QCIL" is an Asia-focused independent private equity investment firm. We provide capital for expansion and merger and acquisition, for both private and public companies. Headquartered in Hong Kong, the firm oversees a broad-based portfolio of investments in sectors ranging from real estates, financial services, infrastructure investment and Agriculture.

Our sister company, Queen Capital Finance Limited (“QCFL”) was founded in 2012 and up to 2018, QCFL successfully completed 30 plus transactions and build value of over US$1 Billion.

Business And Consulting

Our success stems from our team’s ability to build tailor unique financial solutions and execute complex transactions, creating long-term value for our shareholders.

In May 2019, we have signed capital investment commitments from our new investors who commit to invest of not less than US$1 billion in us.

Moving Forward

"With the commitment from our new investors, we will continue to invest in opportunities that reflect our differentiated investment strategy, deep sector expertise, and focus on operational improvement. We look forward to continuing to deliver value for our investors while helping to build great new companies across Asia,"

- said David Chen, Chief Operation Officer for QCIF.

Investment Strategy

QCIL is a pioneer in private investment in technology-enabled industries and financial services in Asia. Queen Capital invests with the strategic and operational insights of an experienced industry participant.

We love to invest in business of development stages. No matter where the business is in its development or well established, we can add value. We work together with management teams and local partners to solve problems and accelerate growth. In all of our investments, we believe in utilizing technology to improve processes and efficiency.

Professional Consulting

We invest only with companies and owners who are receptive to strategic partnership and share our views.

Valuable Ideas

We are extremely hands on with our portfolio companies. We strongly believe that the right opportunity attracts capital.

Industry Experience

We have no problem saying “no” to entrepreneurs with great businesses who do not share our views on partnership, transparency and governance.

Driven by Returns

We are driven by returns, not assets under management. We will not make new investments unless we have the same high conviction as our existing investments.

Management Team

Mr. Luong Chi Sieu (William)
Chief Investment Officer

Mr. Luong becomes the executive director and CEO of QCIL since 2019. He is a serial entrepreneur and an angel investor. Mr. Luong started his career in early 2000 and involved himself in agricultural products trading in South East Asia and then get into the real estate development where he made a fortune. Mr. Luong is a limited partner in 5 VC funds and he is an angel investor in more than 20 promising start-ups, including QCIL.

Mr. Johnny Tang
Director& Chief Finance Officer

Mr. Tang is serving as the executive director and CFO in both QCFL and QCIL. He shares responsibilities for private equity fund investments and direct/co-investments in private companies in Asia Pacific region. Mr. Tang is a certified public accountant (Practising) in Hong Kong and has over 25 years of experience in overseeing the financial matters both in the private companies and public companies.

Mr. David Chen
Chief Operation Officer

Mr. Chen has 30 plus years of investment and management experience in Greater China, and most recently in a RMB investment fund affiliated with one of the China State Owned Enterprise. Mr. Chen joined the board of directors of QCIL in 2018 and is renowned as an expert in Chinese regulations and is responsible for domestic issues related to the structuring and monitoring of portfolio companies in Greater China region.
